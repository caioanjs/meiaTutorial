What is Meiathon?

Answer: Meiathon is an exciting event where people come together to celebrate innovation and collaboration. It’s a marathon for creativity and problem-solving, where participants engage in various activities aimed at driving positive change and creating lasting impact.

How do I participate?

Answer: Participation in Meiathon is simple. All you need to do is register through our website and select the event you wish to join. You can participate as an individual or as part of a team, depending on your preferences.

What are the benefits of attending Meiathon?

Answer: Meiathon offers several benefits including networking with industry professionals, learning new skills, collaborating on creative projects, and the opportunity to win exciting prizes. It’s a great opportunity for personal and professional growth!

Where is the event held?

Answer: Meiathon is held at multiple locations across the world. Check the event page for specific locations and dates. We also offer a virtual option for participants who can’t attend in person.
